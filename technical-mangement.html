Service Technical Management

Technical Management

At Vridhi Maritime Pvt. Ltd , our core competence is the Technical management for ship owners. We aspire to be the market leader in technical Management with emphasis on future business developments and also client satisfaction is our primary concern

Technical Consultancy

Our team of technical experts has many years of experience in the field, who provide accurate and cost effective technical solutions for various technical issues. Depending on the scope of work and workforce needed, we can vary the team structure and tenure on board suitable to our principal’s requirements.

Technical Services

We provide various services for vessels calling Indian ports like annual equipment servicing jobs such as complete LSA / FFA, Gas detector calibration / service, pressure / temp gauge calibrations, crane & gangway load testing / servicing, radars & other navigational equipment service etc or any purifier, boiler, main engine /auxiliary engine maintenance / overhauls etc. We supply stores for deck / engine / cabin / LSA / FFA / medical/ nautical equipments / publications / lubes etc.

Work Shop and repairs

Sea horse is our official partner in our company, having the expertise on following:

  • Removal of Propeller and seized tail shaft for renewal of Stern bush
  • Foundation and installation of Hydraulic power pack
  • Removal of inboard oil seal housing for overhaul
  • Removal of deck provision crane trolley assembly
  • Main engine gear wheel overhaul
  • Fabrication of 3 Pcs 'A’ frames in shaft tunnel for Tail shaft cradle
  • Bow & Bulbous bow damage repairs
  • Replacement of bow thruster
  • Steel repair of collision and piping
  • Steel renewal in water ballast tank
